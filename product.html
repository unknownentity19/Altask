01 / GENERATE

AI website generation that actually understands your business

Describe what you want in a sentence. Altask drafts a real, editable site — hero, features, pricing, FAQ, CTA — all populated with smart defaults and your brand tone. No empty placeholders, no Lorem Ipsum, no waiting for a designer.

  • Trained on 240k production sections, not stock layouts
  • Outputs are always editable — never AI-locked
  • Brand voice fine-tuning per project
02 / EDIT

The drag-and-drop editor that doesn't fight you

Click any text on the canvas to edit it inline. Drag blocks to reorder. Use the inspector for properties. Smart snapping, alignment guides, and 80 levels of undo — like Figma, but the output is a real website.

  • 15+ component types, fully styleable
  • Multiplayer cursors and inline comments
  • Keyboard-first: ⌘D, ⌘↑/↓, ⌘Z all work
03 / SHIP

Hit publish, your site is live in 1.2 seconds

From canvas to a global edge with HTTPS in under two seconds. Custom domains in 2 minutes. Asset optimization, image conversion, font subsetting — all automatic. No deploy scripts, no DNS forms, no SSL emails.

  • 220+ edge locations, median TTFB under 120ms
  • Auto Brotli compression, WebP/AVIF conversion
  • Every publish is a snapshot — instant rollback
