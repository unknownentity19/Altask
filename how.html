Common questions about the flow

Do I have to use the AI? Can I start blank?

Yes — every project supports a blank canvas. The AI is one entry point, not a requirement. Some teams prefer starting from a template or empty canvas.

What happens to my AI-generated content if I edit it?

It becomes regular content. There's no "AI content" tier — every block is a real component the second it lands on the canvas. Edit, move, or delete freely.

Can my team review before I publish?

Yes. Pro and Team plans include staging URLs, inline comments, and an approval flow. Reviewers see the page in its final form, not a build artifact.

How fast is "1.2 seconds" really? What's the catch?

Median first deploy is 1.2s. Larger sites (100+ pages) take longer because of asset processing. Subsequent deploys cache aggressively and stay under 1s for most updates.
